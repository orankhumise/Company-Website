Our Services

Fieldserve provides integrated drone and satellite remote sensing solutions for mineral exploration and geotechnical monitoring.

Prospecting Rights
Applications

Applying for prospecting rights requires a strong technical understanding of the area being targeted and a clear plan for future exploration activities. Fieldserve assists early-stage mining investors throughout this process by helping identify and evaluate areas with mineral potential.

Our work begins by reviewing available mining, geological, and exploration information for the area of interest. We combine these datasets with satellite remote sensing techniques to better understand surface conditions, geological features, and potential mineralisation targets.

The results of these studies are used to support the development of a prospecting works programme, providing the technical foundation required for a robust prospecting rights application.

Drone Geophysical
Surveys

Fieldserve provides high-resolution UAV magnetic surveys for mineral exploration and subsurface investigations. Using a UAV magnetometer and base station, we can efficiently collect geophysical data across large areas while maintaining a closer distance to the ground than conventional airborne surveys.

This approach allows rapid data collection in rugged terrain where ground surveys may be slow, costly, or difficult to perform and helicopter surveys are too costly or unable to provide the required resolution.

We manage the complete survey process, including project planning, data acquisition, processing, quality control, interpretation, and reporting. Our surveys are designed and interpreted by experienced geophysicists and geologists with extensive experience with African geology.

Geotechnical
Monitoring

Mining operations and legacy tailings facilities require continuous monitoring to identify conditions that may indicate developing risks. Fieldserve uses satellite remote sensing technologies to detect deformation, instability, ground movement, seepage indicators, and other geotechnical risk factors.

Our monitoring solutions are particularly valuable for investors evaluating tailings remining opportunities where historical performance and stability are critical considerations.

By analysing both historical and current satellite observations, we provide insights that support risk management and operational planning.
