Fieldserve

Drone & Satellite Surveys for Mineral Exploration & Geotechnical Monitoring

Fieldserve is a specialist remote surveying business that provides data acquisition and analysis for applications in mining and mineral exploration for both investors and technical professionals.

Services

Mineral Exploration Targeting

We conduct desktop studies and analyze satellite imagery to help mining investors assess the mining potential of areas of interest before committing resources for field work and prospecting rights applications.

Drone Geophysical Surveys

We provide exploration geologists and permit or rights holders with airborne magnetic surveys using drones for exploration projects that have progressed past the desktop and surface mapping stages. Our UAV magnetic surveys help project managers identify magnetic anomalies over large and rugged terrain in a fraction of the time required for ground surveys—while offering higher resolution and closer proximity to the surface than manned aircraft.

Satellite Geotechnical Monitoring

We use remote sensing techniques such as InSAR and multispectral processing to assess the stability of legacy and active tailings dams and dumps. We analyze available satellite imagery and provide a historical assessment of risks and developments so that investors can make informed decisions for tailings re-mining projects, or operators can implement continuous monitoring for active deposition.
