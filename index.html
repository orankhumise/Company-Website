FieldServe

Remote Sensing & Geoscience Solutions for Mining and Infrastructure

FieldServe provides advanced geospatial and remote sensing solutions that support mining investors, exploration managers, and geotechnical stakeholders in making informed, data-driven decisions.

Our Services

1. Mineral Exploration Targeting

We support early-stage mining investors in progressing through the mineral exploration journey by providing desk studies and remote sensing analysis to assess the mining potential of an area of interest before any field mapping takes place. This helps reduce uncertainty and improves early-stage decision-making.

2. Drone Geophysics

We assist exploration managers and stakeholders by acquiring high-resolution geophysical data using drones. This enables the generation of potential drill and trenching targets. Drone-based surveys allow us to cover large and uneven terrain efficiently and in a timeous manner, improving exploration productivity and accuracy.

3. Geotechnical Monitoring

We use satellite radar data combined with additional sensor inputs to assess risks associated with legacy tailings storage facilities. This allows investors and stakeholders to evaluate historical risk, track ground deformation, and identify early indicators of structural or environmental risk over time.
